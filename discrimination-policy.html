Non-Discrimination and Anti-Harassment Policy for Goalden Effective Date: May 20, 2024 1. Introduction Goalden is committed to providing an inclusive and welcoming environment for all users. We do not tolerate discrimination or harassment of any kind. This policy outlines our commitment to maintaining a safe and respectful community for everyone. 2. Non-Discrimination Policy We prohibit discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, national origin, genetics, disability, age, or any other characteristic protected by law. This applies to all aspects of our operations, including but not limited to: Access to and use of our app and services Communication and interaction within the Goalden community 3. Anti-Harassment Policy Harassment in any form is not tolerated at Goalden. This includes, but is not limited to: Verbal Harassment: Inappropriate or offensive comments, jokes, or slurs Physical Harassment: Unwanted physical contact or threats of violence Visual Harassment: Display of derogatory or offensive images, messages, or graphics Online Harassment: Cyberbullying, including sending threatening or abusive messages, stalking, or spreading false information 4. Reporting and Response If you experience or witness any form of discrimination or harassment, please report it immediately. You can do this by: Contacting us directly at goaldengirlsrule@gmail.com Using any reporting tools available within the Goalden app We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly and thoroughly. Appropriate actions will be taken to address any violations of this policy. 5. Consequences of Violations Users who violate this policy may face consequences, including but not limited to: Warnings or temporary suspension of account Permanent banning from Goalden Reporting to relevant authorities if necessary 6. Commitment to a Safe Environment We are committed to fostering a community where everyone feels safe, respected, and valued. We continuously work to improve our policies and practices to ensure a positive experience for all users. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update this Non-Discrimination and Anti-Harassment Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the effective date. 8. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Non-Discrimination and Anti-Harassment Policy, please contact us at goaldengirlsrule@gmail.com.
